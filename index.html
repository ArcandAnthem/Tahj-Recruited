Overview Comparison Column Definitions:
  • Overall Fit Score: Composite score (0-100) based on all school factors.
  • Male vs Female Ratio: The undergraduate gender ratio shown as male:female.
  • Black Students (# & %): Count and percentage of Black undergraduate students in the latest reported year.
  • Total Undergrad Population: Total number of undergraduate students in the latest reported year.
  • Average Graduating Salary (USD): Average salary reported by graduates of that school.
  • Pre-Med Med-School Acceptance (%): Percentage of pre-med students accepted to medical school.
  • Conference & Region: Athletic conference and geographic region.
  • Selectivity: Academic admission selectivity level.
  • NFL Players: Number of players from that school recruited to the NFL.
  • Bowl, Conference Titles, National Titles: Football program achievements.
School Choice Priorities Definitions:
  • Tailored Score: Composite score (0-100) calculated from the slider weights chosen by the student.
  • Playing Time Proxy (0-100): Estimated from roster size and conference strength, where smaller rosters and less crowded competition increase opportunity.
  • NFL Dev Proxy (0-100): NFL placement history, normalized to a 0-100 scale.
  • Winning Proxy (0-100): Weighted football success from bowl appearances, conference titles, and national championships.
  • Academic Proxy (0-100): Academic selectivity normalized to the scoring scale.
  • Earnings Proxy (0-100): Graduate salary normalized across the school list.
  • Diversity Proxy (0-100): Non-white student percentage normalized to the scoring scale.
