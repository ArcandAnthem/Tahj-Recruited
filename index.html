Development Score & Column Definitions:
  • Tailored Dev Score: Composite score (0-100) calculated from your six slider weights. Adjust sliders and watch how school rankings change.
  • Playing Time Proxy (0-100): Estimated based on roster size (smaller = more playing time) and conference strength (weaker = more opportunities).
  • NFL Dev Proxy (0-100): Raw count of NFL players from this school, normalized to 0-100 scale.
  • Winning Proxy (0-100): Weighted combination of bowl appearances (50%), conference titles (30%), and national championships (20%), normalized to 0-100.
  • Academic Proxy (0-100): School's academic selectivity level (Very High = 100, High = 80, Moderate = 60, etc.), normalized to 0-100.
  • Earnings Proxy (0-100): Average salary of graduates, normalized across all schools to 0-100 (highest salary = 100, lowest = 0).
  • Diversity Proxy (0-100): Percentage of non-white students on campus, normalized to 0-100.
